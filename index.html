🏋️ Circuit Leg Day Training Manual

Duration: 30–40 min • Goal: Strength • Glutes • Hamstrings • Core

Warm-Up Circuit 1 (3 Rounds) Circuit 2 (2 Rounds) Cool-Down

🔥 Warm-Up (5–7 min)

1️⃣ Dynamic Toe Touches

Target muscles: Hamstrings • Lower back • Calves

How to: Stand tall → bend to touch toes (hold ~3s) → return upright. 10 reps

2️⃣ Forward Lunges with Twist

Target muscles: Quads • Glutes • Core/Obliques

How to: Step forward into lunge → rotate toward front leg → push back. 10 each leg

3️⃣ 90/90 Hip Stretch with Rotation

Target muscles: Glutes • Hip rotators • Hip flexors

How to: Sit in 90/90 → hinge toward front shin → rotate toward rear leg. 8–10 each side

💪 Circuit 1 — 3 Rounds (rest 60–90s)

4️⃣ Goblet Squat with Side Steps (on Plate) — 10 reps

Target muscles: Quadriceps • Glutes • Core

How to: Stand with small plate beneath feet. Step left → squat, step right → squat, then both feet on plate → squat.

5️⃣ Cable Romanian Deadlift (Cable RDL) — 12 reps

Target muscles: Hamstrings • Glutes • Lower back

How to: Face cable, bar near shins. Hinge at hips (knees soft, back flat) → lower until hamstrings stretch → drive hips forward to stand tall.

6️⃣ Weighted Jackknife Sit-Up — 15 reps

Target muscles: Rectus abdominis • Hip flexors

How to: Lie flat holding a plate overhead. Raise arms & legs to meet in the middle. Return controlled.

🧱 Circuit 2 — 2 Rounds (rest 60–90s)

7️⃣ Bench Barbell Hamstring Curl — 10 reps

Target muscles: Hamstrings (all heads) • Glutes (assist) • Calves (assist)

How to: Lie face down on flat bench with barbell held between feet. Curl heels toward glutes; lower slowly.

8️⃣ Weighted Walking Lunges — 10–12 steps each leg

Target muscles: Quads • Glutes • Hamstrings

How to: Hold dumbbells or plate. Step forward into deep lunge, alternate legs, full range of motion.

🧘 Cool-Down (5 min)

  • Seated Hamstring Stretch — 30 s/leg
  • Standing Quad Stretch — 30 s/leg
  • Figure-4 Glute Stretch — 30 s/leg
  • Hip Flexor Stretch — 30 s/leg

Trainer Notes: Tempo 2s down/1s up. Keep spine neutral on Cable RDL and curls. Squeeze glutes at the top. Form > weight.
